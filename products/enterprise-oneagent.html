Capabilities

One front door to your enterprise.

OneAgent doesn't replace your systems — it sits in front of them. It understands the question, finds the answer with the right permissions, and takes the next action with a clear audit trail.

Cross-system search

One natural-language search across HRMS, ITSM, DMS, ERP, CRM, email and documents.

Role-based answers

Every user sees only what they're authorized to — permissions are enforced on each answer.

Workflow initiation

Raise tickets, requests and approvals across systems — drafted by the agent, confirmed by a human.

Grounded & cited

Answers come from approved sources with references and confidence — no black-box guesses.

Connectors

Secure connectors and APIs link Microsoft and non-Microsoft systems into one layer.

Audit & control

Every query, answer and action is logged, with human approval on anything sensitive.

How it works

Understand, retrieve, act — under control.

01 — Ask

Understand intent

Interpret the request in plain language and identify which systems hold the answer.

02 — Retrieve

Search with permissions

Query connected systems within the user's access rights and gather grounded context.

03 — Reason

Answer & cite

Compose a grounded answer with source references and a confidence score.

04 — Act

Initiate & route

Draft the workflow, route for approval where needed, and log every step.

OneAgent is governed by design: role-based access, approved sources, human-in-the-loop approvals and full audit — deployable in cloud, hybrid or on-prem.
