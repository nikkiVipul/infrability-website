Capabilities

Built for the realities of banking documents.

Cheques come in many templates and two scripts. Cheque Intelligence reads them on-prem, extracts the exact fields banking operations need, scores its confidence on each one, and gets better over time from the corrections your team makes.

Field-level extraction

Cheque number, date, payee (Arabic & English), amount in numbers and words, and account number.

Confidence per field

Every value carries a confidence score — set a threshold for straight-through processing versus review.

Template & ROI aware

Region-based extraction tuned to your cheque layouts — including Qatar-region and bilingual templates.

Human review queue

Items below your threshold route to validation with the original image — and corrections are captured.

Continuous improvement

Captured corrections become labeled data for retraining — measurable accuracy uplift on your hard cases.

API-first & on-prem

Structured JSON and CSV via REST to core banking, ECM/DMS and workflow engines — running fully inside your network.

How it works

From scanned cheque to validated data.

01 — Ingest

Upload or batch

Process scanned images or PDFs from your capture systems, with optional pre-processing.

02 — Detect

Template & ROI

Identify the cheque format and locate the regions for each required field.

03 — Extract

OCR + post-process

Arabic/English OCR with cleaning, validation rules and a confidence score per field.

04 — Decide

Auto-approve or review

High confidence goes straight through; low confidence routes to review and captures corrections.

You set the threshold. Straight-through where the model is confident, human review where it isn't — and every correction feeds the improvement loop. Nothing low-certainty is passed downstream unchecked.
