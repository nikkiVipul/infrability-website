Capabilities

Every call, analyzed — not just sampled.

Instead of QA-ing a handful of calls, VoCallIQ listens to all of them — turning raw audio into transcripts, sentiment, themes and quality scores you can act on at scale.

Transcription

Accurate speech-to-text across languages, accents and telephony audio quality.

Diarization

Separate agent and customer speakers with timing, turn-taking and talk-over detection.

Sentiment & emotion

Track sentiment across each call and over time, by queue, agent and topic.

Keyword clustering

Discover recurring themes, drivers and emerging issues without predefined tags.

Quality monitoring

Auto-score calls against your QA scorecard for compliance, script and empathy adherence.

CX insights & dashboards

Turn call data into trends and dashboards for CX, operations and leadership.

How it works

From audio to action.

01 — Ingest

Ingest calls

Connect call recordings from your contact center, batch or near-real-time.

Transcribe & split

Apply sentiment, QA scoring and theme clustering across every call.
