Capabilities

From regulatory text to a remediation plan.

RegNexus reads the regulations the way your compliance team does — then does the cross-referencing, gap-finding and evidence-gathering at machine speed, with a human in control of every decision.

Regulation ingestion

Ingest circulars, acts, standards and guidelines as PDFs or feeds — structured into clauses and obligations.

Policy comparison

Map each regulatory obligation to the matching clause in your internal policies and procedures.

Gap analysis

Surface unmet, partial and outdated obligations with severity and a clear explanation of the gap.

Remediation plans

Draft action plans with suggested owners, target dates and the policy changes required to close each gap.

Audit-ready evidence

Every finding links back to the exact regulatory clause and policy section, with full logging for review.

Continuous monitoring

Track regulatory change and re-run impact analysis when a new circular or policy version lands.
