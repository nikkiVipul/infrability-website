Customer Story · Banking

Cheque processing, automated and kept on-prem.

The challenge. A bank needed to process 2,000–3,000 cheques a day, in Arabic and English — without sending sensitive financial documents outside its own environment.

What we built. Infrability deployed Cheque Intelligence on-prem. It reads payee, courtesy and legal amounts, dates and account fields directly from the bank's own cheque formats, scores its confidence on every field, and routes only the uncertain cases to a human reviewer — improving with each correction.
