AIVortex Intelligence Layer

One AI layer across your enterprise systems.

AIVortex is Infrability's enterprise AI engineering layer for building secure agents, copilots, document intelligence systems, regulatory intelligence platforms and workflow automation. It connects business systems, understands enterprise documents, reasons over approved knowledge, executes controlled workflows, and keeps humans in control for sensitive decisions.
